Leroy's Charity Projects

Here at Leroy's we have dedicated ourselves to not only source our products localy but to also donate 100% of our profits to multiple charitable organizations in not only just Ketchum but also throughout the Wood River Valley. Some of these organization are listed bellow:

Ketchum:

  • Blaine County Schools Foundation
  • The Community School
  • Ketchum Parks Department
  • Sun Valley Ski Education Foundation

Hailey:

  • Pioneer Montessori School
  • Blaine County Charitable Fund
  • Hailey Fire Department
