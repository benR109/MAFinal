About Us:

The ice cream hub of Ketchum, LeRoy’s is located in Ketchum’s Town Square (across the street from Atkinson’s Market and behind Starbuck’s). This easy to find, easy to love ice cream shack will bring you back again and again so get in line and enjoy what LeRoy’s has to offer. LeRoy’s operates with local goodness in mind – all of our ice cream is made at one of our favorite local organic creameries, CloverLeaf Creamery in Buhl, ID.

Leroy’s Ice Cream is more than just a local favorite; it’s a vibrant reflection of Ketchum’s unique culture and welcoming spirit. Situated in the heart of town at the iconic Town Square, Leroy’s embodies the warmth and charm that define this mountain community. Surrounded by bustling shops, lively events, and the picturesque backdrop of the Wood River Valley, the little ice cream shack is a cornerstone of connection for residents and visitors alike. Whether it’s families gathering after school, hikers stopping in for a treat after a day on the trails, or friends catching up under the summer sun, Leroy’s is where moments of joy and community come together.

At the very center of Ketchum’s vibrant social life, Leroy’s Ice Cream is more than a destination—it’s a tradition. Its bright and cheerful atmosphere draws people in, while the quality of its locally crafted ice cream keeps them coming back. Leroy’s is not just a place to grab a cone; it’s a place where memories are made, stories are shared, and the essence of Ketchum’s laid-back, friendly culture thrives. For anyone passing through this charming town, Leroy’s is a must-visit spot that captures the heart and soul of Ketchum in every scoop.

Our Mission:

In addition to ice cream, the menu also offers frozen yogurt, sundaes, root beer & orange floats, and chocolate-covered bananas. And while the ice cream is fabulous and the service top notch, the core of our buisness here at LeRoy’s Ice Cream is that 100% of the profits are split amongst the following organizations in the Wood River Valley:

  • Blaine County Schools Foundation
  • The Community School
  • Ketchum Parks Department
  • Pioneer Montessori School
  • Sun Valley Ski Education Foundation

Leroy's is truly a guilt-free ice cream eating experience!!
