Job Opportunities

At Leroy's Ice Cream, we believe in creating a fun, inclusive, and rewarding environment for our employees. We're always on the lookout for enthusiastic individuals to join our team. If you’re passionate about customer service and love ice cream, check out our job openings below!

Current Openings

  • Ice Cream Server (Part-Time): Join our friendly team and serve our delicious ice cream to customers. Flexible hours, competitive pay, and a great work environment!
  • Shift Supervisor (Full-Time): Take on a leadership role at Leroy's. Responsibilities include managing staff, maintaining inventory, and ensuring smooth daily operations.
  • Event Coordinator: Plan and manage community events and special promotions for Leroy’s Ice Cream Shop. Creativity and excellent organizational skills required.

Why Work With Us?

  • Flexible schedules
  • Opportunities for growth
  • Discounts on all our products
  • Be part of a company that gives back to the community

How to Apply

To apply, please send your resume and a brief cover letter to leroysicecreamsv@gmail.com. You can also drop off your application at our shop during regular business hours.
